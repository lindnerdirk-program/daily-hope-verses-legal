Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Daily Hope Verses handles information in connection with its small creator workflow and TikTok upload authorization.

Project Overview

Daily Hope Verses is a small creator project that shares short inspirational videos based on Bible verses, ancient wisdom, philosophy and timeless reflections.

The related creator tool is used by the project owner to prepare short-form videos and upload them to supported platforms, including TikTok.

Use of TikTok Authorization

Daily Hope Verses uses TikTok Login/OAuth only for the creator account that owns or manages the project.

The authorization is used to prepare and upload short videos to TikTok as drafts or in-app uploads, so the creator can review and publish them manually in the TikTok app.

Daily Hope Verses is not affiliated with or endorsed by TikTok.

Information We Handle

  • TikTok authorization data may be used only to complete authorized creator uploads.
  • Access tokens may be stored locally on the creator's computer when needed for the upload workflow.
  • The project does not collect public TikTok user profiles, comments, followers or analytics from other TikTok users.

No Sale of Personal Data

Daily Hope Verses does not sell, rent or trade personal data. Data is not used for advertising, tracking, profiling or resale.

No cookies, analytics scripts or tracking pixels are used on this website.
