Privacy Policy

Daily Hope Verses is a private desktop content creation tool used to prepare and upload short inspirational videos.

The app uses TikTok authorization only to allow the creator to upload their own video content to TikTok. The app does not sell, share, rent, or use TikTok user data for advertising.

The app may store TikTok access tokens locally on the creator's computer in order to complete authorized uploads. These tokens are not shared with third parties.

The app does not collect public user profiles, comments, followers, or analytics from TikTok users. It is only used by the app owner to upload their own short-form video content.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the app owner.
