Project Overview

Daily Hope Verses is a small creator project focused on short inspirational videos based on Bible verses, ancient wisdom, philosophy and timeless reflections.

The related desktop workflow is intended for the project creator to prepare and upload their own video content to supported platforms, including TikTok.

TikTok Upload Workflow

TikTok Login/OAuth is used only for the creator account. The workflow helps prepare and upload videos to TikTok as drafts or in-app uploads, so the creator can review and publish them manually in the TikTok app.

Daily Hope Verses does not claim to be an official TikTok service and is not affiliated with or endorsed by TikTok.

Creator Responsibility

The creator is responsible for reviewing all videos, captions, hashtags and related metadata before publishing.
